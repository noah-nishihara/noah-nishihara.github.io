Neko95 Maze

A Windows 95 3D Maze-inspired cat adventure with brick corridors, a roaming rat, flip rocks, a Smiley exit, and an optional screensaver mode.

  • 1 Classic: first-person maze. W/S move, A/D or arrows turn.
  • 2 Cat: third-person cat. WASD camera-relative, mouse drag rotates camera.
  • 3 Screensaver: the maze walks itself, collecting charms before heading to the Smiley exit.
